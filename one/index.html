One Telecommunications started in 2021 One Scholars Program, aiming to support talented students pursue a better future. Well-educated workforce of tomorrow depends on qualitative education today. We acknowledge that to remain competitive in an ever-changing global marketplace requires the steady influx of new talent. By sponsoring scholarship programs, we can encourage and develop the next generation of employees and executives who will lead the market to greater successes.

One Scholars gives the opportunity to 15 students per year to attend one of the Bachelor programs of AUBG and RIT Kosovo, with significant reduction on tuition fee.
